# Codex CLI Hands-On Labs -- Student & Instructor Guide

> **Course:** AI Agents for the Next Generation (2.5 days)
> **Audience:** High school and early college students
> **Tool:** OpenAI Codex CLI

---

## Overview

Labs are where students go from watching to DOING. Each lab builds on the
demos from the same day. Students work at their own machines (or in pairs)
with Codex CLI installed and authenticated.

**Instructor role during labs:** Walk the room, answer questions, celebrate
wins, and help students who are stuck. Resist the urge to give answers --
redirect with questions: "What prompt could you try?"

## Before Labs Begin -- Instructor Checklist

- [ ] Every student machine has `codex` installed and authenticated
- [ ] Each machine has a working terminal (VS Code terminal works great)
- [ ] The mystery project zip/folder is prepared (run `mystery-project-setup.sh`)
- [ ] Lab handouts are printed or shared digitally
- [ ] You have walked through each lab yourself at least once
- [ ] Backup hotspot is ready in case Wi-Fi drops

---

## Lab Structure (Same for Every Lab)

1. **Intro (5 min):** Instructor sets the scene, explains the goal
2. **Work time:** Students work independently or in pairs
3. **Check-ins:** Instructor circulates, offers hints, not answers
4. **Wrap-up (5 min):** 2-3 students share what they found or built

---

## Helping Stuck Students -- The Hint Ladder

Before giving a student the answer, try these in order:

1. **Re-read the prompt:** "Did you include all four parts of WHAT-WHERE-HOW-VERIFY?"
2. **Check the sandbox:** "What mode are you in? Do you need to change it?"
3. **Suggest iteration:** "What if you asked Codex to explain what went wrong?"
4. **Pair them up:** "Ask your neighbor how they approached it."
5. **Give a starting prompt:** Only as a last resort.

---

## Grading Philosophy

These labs are NOT graded on correctness. Focus on:
- **Effort:** Did the student engage with the tool?
- **Creativity:** Did they try interesting prompts or approaches?
- **Reflection:** Can they explain what happened and why?
- **Growth:** Did they get better at prompting over the three days?
